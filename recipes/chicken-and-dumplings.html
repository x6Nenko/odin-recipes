Super Easy Chicken and Dumplings

Description

The recipe for Super Easy Chicken and Dumplings from Allrecipes provides a comforting and hearty dish that is simple to prepare. This recipe begins by simmering boneless chicken with vegetables and seasonings to create a flavorful broth. Then, fluffy biscuit-like dumplings are dropped into the simmering broth and cooked until they become light and tender. The result is a warm and satisfying bowl of chicken and dumplings, with tender chicken, flavorful vegetables, and pillowy dumplings soaking up the delicious broth. This recipe is perfect for those seeking a quick and delicious comfort meal that will warm both the body and soul.

Ingredients

  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 3 (14 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 3 cups shredded cooked chicken meat
  • 2 (10 ounce) cans refrigerated biscuit dough

Steps

  1. Stir condensed soup, chicken broth, and shredded chicken together in a large saucepan over medium-high heat until it begins to simmer.
  2. Cut each biscuit into quarters, and gently stir into the simmering soup.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until biscuits are no longer doughy in the center, 10 to 15 minutes.
